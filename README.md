# Motorola Smartphone Sales Performance Dashboard

A comprehensive data analytics and business intelligence dashboard designed to monitor, track, and analyze smartphone sales performance across diverse regional markets. This interactive tool transforms raw transactional sales data into actionable commercial insights, enabling data-driven decision-making for inventory, marketing, and revenue optimization.

---

## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales Revenue:** 308M managed volume across the reporting cycle.
* **Total Units Sold:** 8K smartphone units successfully distributed.
* **Total Transactions:** 2K independent processing sales completed.
* **Average Transaction Value:** A healthy 41K average per ticket baseline.

---

## 📊 Analytical Visualizations & Deep Dives

### 1. Geographic Revenue Breakdown
* **Total Sales by City:** An interactive geospatial map layer plotting market density and revenue contributions across major metropolitan hubs (including Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Ludhiana).

### 2. Time-Series & Temporal Analysis
* **Sum of Units Sold by Day:** High-frequency line chart tracking daily cyclical volume fluctuations to identify structural demand shifts.
* **Total Sales by Day Name:** Revenue distribution mapping across the week, highlighting peak sales periods (Friday leading with 48M volume).

### 3. Operational & Brand Diagnostics
* **Brand Matrix Leaderboard:** Structured tabular breakdown tracking performance across key market players (Apple, OnePlus, Samsung, Vivo, Xiaomi) across sales revenue, quantities, and transaction counts.
* **Total Sales by Mobile Model:** Horizontal bar chart highlighting premium top-performing individual SKUs (iPhone SE leading at 25M).

### 4. Consumer Behavior Insights
* **Transactions by Payment Method:** Donut chart evaluating financial transaction channels, indicating strong ecosystem diversity across UPI (30.09%), Cash (25.3%), Debit Cards (24.14%), and Credit Cards.
* **Customer Ratings Profile:** Funnel/horizontal chart mapping feedback distribution scores to gauge overall product and post-purchase satisfaction.

---

## 🛠️ Interactive Capabilities & Filtering Controls

* **Global Date Slicers:** Multi-select vertical month-by-month timeline filter panel spanning January through December.
* **Granular Data Toggles:** Real-time dropdown filter controls across Mobile Model, Payment Method, Brand, and specific Day Names.
* **Visual Identity:** Modern corporate user interface utilizing a cohesive navy blue, teal, and slate palette designed for high executive readability.
